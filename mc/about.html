About us

A terracotta and handicraft botique.

Handicraft is a wonderful God given art that involves a lot of skill and beautiful creativity. The intricate art works are completely hand made and it is said to have it's origin from the Indus Valley Civilization and very closely related to our own India.

We at MOTHERLAND CRAFTS are in so much love with Handicrafts and the art form , that we strive to bring out the various mind-blowing artworks from all over the country to your doorstep. Another beautiful facet of these handicraft items are that these are 100% Eco-friendly that augurs well with our Go Green motto.

We aim at providing our patrons with a wide variety of unique collections that they can gift to their special ones and turn their moments extra special. Motherland Crafts will be your one stop destination to decorate your homes and make it classic. We aim at supporting the skillful artisans of country by taking their Handicraft to every corner possible.
